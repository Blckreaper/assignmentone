Twelve Pillars

  • Chapter 4 - Charlie speaks to Micheal about how setting a goal is important because of what it requires of you to achieve it. He further expounded stating that Setting goals entails choosing a destination and creating a strategy to get there. You may create the future you want by setting goals that challenge you and force you to think and act differently. Don't wait for others to assign you goals.

  • Chapter 5 -Charlie continued to impart knowledge on Micheal regarding that there is only one excellent opportunity out of thousands that exist every day. Either the sorrow of regret or the pain of discipline must be endured by each and every one of us. Discipline weighs a few ounces, while regret weighs several tons. The opportunities that best fit your long-term objectives are the ones you should focus on each day. Don&apost just go through the day, get from the day. Don&apost spend you time on urgent, spend your time on what is important. The design of our lives must be done by us.

  • Chapter 6 - Three weeks after their intial meeting Charlie and Micheal met at a pool behind a house. They had a sit down and Charlie contiues to advise Micheal on the twelve pillars starting with avoid easy company; you won't advance. Go where there are high standards and performance requirements. Consider the following: Who am I surrounded by? Why are they having such an impact on me? What are they making me read? What are they making me say? Where are they taking me? What are they making me think? The most crucial question is: What do they envision me as? Then, ponder whether or not that is acceptable. Convenience friends. This idea has some appeal. Is it simpler to maintain existing friendships than to make new ones? Successful people seek connections with like-minded individuals who will push, challenge, and inspire them to improve. You must be appealing in order to draw attractive people. not actually, but mentally and in terms of who you are. People you associate with have a big impact on your mindset.

  • The final last lesson Charlie gave to Micheal in this particular scene was your livelihood will come from formal schooling. Your own education will bring you great wealth. You educate yourself by doing it yourself. It is what you discover along the journey that will keep you evolving and expanding. Being focused on continual learning can help you stand out from the crowd because most individuals don't do it. Wealth begins with education. Health begins with education. Spirituality begins with learning. The magical process all starts with searching and learning. The majority of life is experienced after formal education. Therefore, the remaining steps are up to you. The books must be read by you. The best learning resources available to people today have been and will be books.
